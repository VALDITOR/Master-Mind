HOW TO PLAY:
BEFORE THE GAME:
• To start the pre-game setup, press the START button on the main menu.
• To start the pre-game setup, press the START button on the main menu.
• The first step is to enter your name; remember you cannot leave the field empty.
• The second step is to select the 4 colors you want to play with; remember you cannot repeat the same color or leave any of the fields blank.
• The final step in the pre-configuration is to select one of the three difficulty levels in which you want to play.
DIFFICULTY CHOICES:
There are three difficulty levels; here are their names and characteristics:
• The first level, called BEGINNER, is the easiest of the three and has 10 attempts per game.
• The second level, called ADVANCED, is the intermediate level of difficulty and has 6 attempts per game.
• The last and most challenging level is called MASTER MIND, which only has 4 attempts. Good luck if you decide to give it a try.
GAME:
• The game consists of guessing the secret combination of four colors before running out of attempts.
• To paint a square, select a color from the left panel and then click on the square you want to paint. Remember that the game is played row by row, representing the attempts, so you can only paint in the row you are in.
• Once you've painted a row, click the CHECK button to check the result. On the right side of the board, there is a ball for each square in the row. If you have guessed the color and position correctly, it will be painted in purple. If you have guessed the color correctly but not the position, it will be painted in black. If you haven't guessed either, it will remain white.
• Once you have guessed the combination correctly, you will win the game.
